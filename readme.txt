Embed Light Field Photos makes it easy to include photos from the www.lytro.com/living-pictures website in your posts and pages.

== Description ==

You can easily add the photos from the lytro.com website to your posts and pages. All you have to do is copy the URL of the photo and drop it into your post, the same as you do with Youtube videos (e.g. http://www.lytro.com/living-pictures/198). The url is automatically changed so the photo is displayed on your blog.

== Installation ==

Upload the Embed Light-Field Photos plugin to your blog, Activate it and you are done.
